# User journey: recruiter evaluation

## 1. Arrival

- **Does:** Opens the portfolio from an application.
- **Thinks:** “What role is this candidate targeting, and are they available?”
- **Feels:** Time-constrained.
- **Opportunity:** Show target roles and Summer 2027 availability immediately.

## 2. Evidence scan

- **Does:** Reads experience and opens selected projects.
- **Thinks:** “Can they work with the systems our team uses?”
- **Feels:** Interested if concrete technologies and outcomes are easy to scan.
- **Opportunity:** Use a clear timeline and compact project cards.

## 3. Decision

- **Does:** Opens the resume or starts contact.
- **Thinks:** “Do I have enough evidence to move forward?”
- **Feels:** Confident when resume and email are always easy to reach.
- **Success:** Resume view or email initiated.
